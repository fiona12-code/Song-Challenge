Jowi by Coster Ojwang

aka "The Fisherman"

Details

Song: Jowi

Artist: Coster Ojwang

Genre: Luo Music

Language: Luo

Location: Kenya

Release Year: 2024

Music video

The song is a beautiful blend of traditional and modern sounds,
showcasing the rich culture and heritage of the Luo community in Kenya.
The song is about a buffalo ("Jowi") and a strong farmer.
The farmer is being told that as much as he is strong and works hard,
he should be careful not to mess with the buffalo when the buffalo comes to raid his farm.
This is because the buffalo is a stronger animal capable of charging at him
and injuring him in the process.
The lyrics are heartfelt and resonate with me, because it makes me connect with my Luo heritage.
In a society where many people are losing touch with their roots,
this song serves as a reminder of the importance of embracing and celebrating our culture.
